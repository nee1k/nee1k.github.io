Sales Analytics for Chemical Products

Project Overview

I conducted a monthly chemical sales forecast at the SKU level across nine regions: the forecasting framework combined time series analysis and heuristic models to project sales trends. Time series analysis thoroughly examined historical sales data and extracted insights to inform forecasting. Additionally, heuristic models incorporated domain-specific knowledge and regional factors influencing chemical sales, contributing to a more nuanced prediction. This approach aimed to enhance the accuracy and reliability of the forecasting process. The results gave decision-makers valuable insights for strategic planning and resource allocation across diverse regional landscapes by tailoring the methodology to quantitative analysis and industry-specific expertise.
